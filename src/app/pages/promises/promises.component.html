Understanding Promise Status Categories

Each promise from the manifesto has been categorized into one of the following five categories based on its current status:

  1. Fulfilled: Fully implemented promises that meet the original commitment.
  2. Violated: Promises disregarded or contradicted by opposing actions.
  3. In Progress: Promises currently under active implementation but not yet completed.
  4. Not Implemented: Promises for which no visible steps or actions have been taken.
  5. Partly Implemented: Promises partially fulfilled, with significant portions unaddressed.
  6. Not Verified Yet: This category includes promises awaiting updates, independent audits,
    or further implementation steps for conclusive evaluation.
